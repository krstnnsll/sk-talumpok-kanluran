Who We Are

The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) of Barangay Talumpok Kanluran is composed of dedicated youth leaders committed to promoting transparency, participation, and progress within our community. We believe in empowering young people to be active agents of change.

Our Vision

A progressive, united, and youth-driven barangay where every young individual is empowered to contribute to sustainable development and good governance.

Our Mission

To create inclusive programs that promote education, health, livelihood, sports, environmental care, and leadership training while upholding transparency, accountability, and integrity in serving the youth and community.
